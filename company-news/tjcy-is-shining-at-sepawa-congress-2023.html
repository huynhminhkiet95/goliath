GOLIATH is shining at SEPAWA CONGRESS 2023!

Oct. 28, 2023

Share:

SEPAWA CONGRESS 2023

 

 

The SEPAWA CONGRESS stands as a pivotal gathering for the European detergents/cleansers, cosmetics, and fragrance industry each year.It is held during Oct 25 - Oct 27, 2023.It is in Sonnenallee 225, 12057 Berlin, Germany.


The comprehensive lecture program delves into various facets of these industries, featuring over 150 engaging presentations and poster displays. International experts seize the opportunity to disseminate their cutting-edge research findings and groundbreaking developments.

 

In the exhibition area, more than 300 companies showcase their newest products, trends, and extensive expertise, providing a firsthand look at the industry's advancements.


To complement the enriching experience, the highly anticipated After Event and numerous networking opportunities create a well-rounded congress framework. These events not only foster business connections but also facilitate the expansion of both professional and personal networks.

 

Meet GOLIATH—Your Trusted Partner


 

 

GOLIATH's presence at SEPAWA CONGRESS 2023, stationed at booth F850, was met with an overwhelming response. The booth became a focal point, drawing a significant number of international visitors keen on exploring our products and engaging in discussions about potential transactions.

 

The warm reception and high level of interest from attendees, especially foreign visitors, underscored the relevance and appeal of GOLIATH's offerings in the chemicals industry. Our team engaged in numerous insightful conversations, addressing inquiries about products and negotiating potential deals. The vibrant atmosphere at the booth reflected the dynamic exchange of ideas and information, contributing to the success of our participation in the congress.

 

As the event unfolded, GOLIATH continued to solidify its position as a key player in the industry, forging valuable connections and partnerships that extend beyond borders. The interactive and collaborative nature of the SEPAWA CONGRESS 2023 provided an ideal platform for GOLIATH to showcase its expertise and explore new avenues for growth in the global market.

 

        

 

This Is Us, GOLIATH.

At GOLIATH, we specialize in chemicals that play a vital role in every facet of our daily lives. Our unwavering commitment revolves around understanding customer needs and delivering value to both our upstream and downstream partners.

 

As a professional trading company, GOLIATH Group excels in industrial chemicals, active pharmaceutical ingredients & intermediates, food & feed additives, personal care, and agricultural chemicals. With a diverse portfolio encompassing over 1,000 products, we offer comprehensive one-stop services. From expert procurement and sales to efficient marketing, warehousing and logistics, and stringent quality assurance QA/QC, GOLIATH Group is dedicated to providing seamless support to both domestic and international customers.
