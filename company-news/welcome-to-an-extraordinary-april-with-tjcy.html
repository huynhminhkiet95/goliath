Welcome to an extraordinary April with TJCY!

Apr. 03, 2024

Share:

Dear partners and friends:


Get ready to embark on an exciting journey of innovation and discovery with TJCY as we prepare to host three blockbuster exhibitions this April:


EXPOFARMA

Date: April 17-19, 2024

Location: World Trade Center, Mexico City, Mexico

Booth number: 1264

Since its founding in 1966, it has brought together the most distinguished professionals and students of the industry in Mexico. It is a reliable institution with international recognition and plays an important role and influence in Mexico's scientific, technological, and pharmaceutical regulatory work.

In-Cosmetics Global

Dates: April 16-18, 2024

Location: Level 1. Palace of Versailles, Paris, France

Booth number: 1C20

in-cosmetics Global is the world's leading event for personal care ingredients. This event celebrates the very best in cosmetic science, uniting outstanding suppliers of personal care ingredients (exhibitors) with proven manufacturers of finished products (visitors) to inspire the cutting-edge cosmetics of the future.

 

Global Ingredients Show

Date: April 23-25, 2024

Location: Pavilion 2, Hall 11, Crocus Expo, Moscow, Russia

Booth number: A218

Global Ingredients Exhibition is a special business platform for the Russian ingredients market with a history of more than 25 years, bringing together producers, suppliers of ingredients and additives, and their customers.


Join us at these important events as we unveil the latest chemical innovations and solutions designed to revolutionize the industry. Work with our knowledgeable team to explore potential collaborations and discover the endless possibilities our products offer.Mark your calendar and be sure to visit our booth to immerse yourself in a world of innovation, expertise, and opportunity. Let us shape the future of the chemical industry together!



We eagerly await your visit and look forward to fruitful discussions and mutually beneficial partnerships.


warm regards,

Tianjin Chengyi International Trading Co. Ltd

About TJCY

TJCY Group is a professional trading company in industrial chemicals, pharmaceutical raw materials, and intermediates, food and feed additives, personal care, and agricultural chemicals. It has five segments and more than 1,000 products. Focusing on customer needs and creating value for upstream and downstream partners is what TJCY has been striving for.
