GOLIATH: Excellent and complete supply chain and advanced laboratory technology

Jan. 29, 2024

Share:

At Tianjin Chengyi International Trading Co. Ltd, we have always been committed to ensuring excellent product quality by establishing a strict supplier management system. We are deeply aware of the critical importance of the supply chain to product quality, so our quality commitment begins with the selection and evaluation of suppliers and is based on compliance with globally recognized quality management system standards to ensure that our products are always at the forefront of the industry leading position.


 

First of all, our selection of suppliers is carefully considered. We strictly require suppliers to comply with a series of standards, including but not limited to GMP (Good Manufacturing Practice), ISO (International Organization for Standardization), FSSC (Food Safety System Certification) and other industry standards. This means our suppliers are required to achieve the highest level of compliance in production and supply chain management. Our supplier qualification program ensures tailored review and assessment processes for different product types. This ensures strict control over our suppliers and provides a reliable guarantee of superior quality products.


 

For GOLIATH company, quality is our lifeline. We know that excellent quality is the key to customer satisfaction. To achieve this goal, we spare no effort in laboratory technology. We insist on using advanced testing equipment and have an experienced professional team. Our QC (Quality Control) laboratory is equipped with the latest instruments and equipment, such as Shimadzu AAS (Atomic Absorption Spectrometer), Thermo ICP-OES (Inductively Coupled Plasma Spectrometer), Mettler Toledo KF Moisture Analyzer, etc., for Conduct key tests such as routine component analysis, moisture testing, and metal impurity testing. The use of these advanced equipment ensures that we have a comprehensive and accurate understanding of product quality, and also provides a high level of quality assurance for our products.

 

Our laboratory team is the backbone of GOLIATH's exceptional quality. Team members not only have deep professional and technical knowledge, but also continuously learn and innovate. With a high sense of responsibility and professionalism, they ensure that every test is accurate and reliable. Through these sophisticated inspection processes, we are able to quickly identify and correct potential problems, ensuring our products meet the highest standards of excellence.


At GOLIATH, we firmly believe that our customers' demand for superior quality coincides with our mission. We are not only committed to providing high-quality products, but also committed to continuously improving our quality management system to provide customers with the best services. We continuously pursue excellence through innovation, technology investment and strict quality control. Supplier management and laboratory technology are key pillars in achieving this goal and reflect GOLIATH's unwavering commitment to quality excellence.


 

At GOLIATH, our quality commitment is not just a slogan, but also our responsibility and commitment to our customers. Our products are not only synonymous with quality, but also a reflection of customer trust. Through our efforts, GOLIATH has become a trustworthy brand, and we will continue to work tirelessly to provide customers with better and more reliable products and services. We believe that through continued efforts, GOLIATH will continue to maintain a leading position in the industry and create more value for customers.
