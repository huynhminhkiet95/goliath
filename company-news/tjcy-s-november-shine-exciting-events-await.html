GOLIATH's November Shine: Exciting Events Await!

Nov. 06, 2023

Share:

 

November is just around the corner, and it brings three exciting international trade shows set to showcase the latest in the world of agriculture and chemicals. GOLIATH is proud to participate in these events, where they will exhibit their cutting-edge products and solutions. Here's a glimpse of what to expect:

 

Expo AgroAlimentaria Guanajuato

 

  

Date: November 7-10, 2023

Location: Irapuato, Mexico

Booth: 1130 (Indoor Area)

Expo AgroAlimentaria Guanajuato has been a renowned international agribusiness event since 1996. With a focus on "Business, Technology, and Development," this event attracts participants from over 52 countries. The sprawling 62-hectare venue showcases the agri-food sector, featuring high-tech greenhouses, demonstration plots, and innovations in the latest indoor and outdoor exhibition area. What sets this event apart is its ability to facilitate crucial B2B meetings, making it a comprehensive event for the global agri-food industry.

 

Yugagro - Russia's Premier Agricultural Trade Show

 

 

Date: November 21-24, 2023

Location: Russia

Booth: hall2-J

Yugagro is Russia's premier international agricultural trade show, featuring over 600 exhibitors from Russia and around the globe. The exhibited companies specialize in agricultural machinery, equipment, and materials for crop production. In 2021, the event drew 18,187 visitors from 75 Russian regions and 39 countries, all seeking new products and services to enhance their businesses. With nearly three decades of prominence in Russian crop production, Yugagro remains a pivotal event in the agricultural calendar.

 

Pesticide products include Insecticides, fungicides, herbicides, plant growth regulators, and botanical pesticides.

 

ABRAFATI Show - Latin America's Leading Coatings Event

 

 

 

Date: November 21-23, 2023

Location: Brazil

Booth: H10

The ABRAFATI Show has a rich history of stimulating innovation, disseminating content and knowledge, and providing opportunities for building solid relationships and driving business growth. This event is more than just participating as an exhibitor for three days; it's about being at the forefront of the market by increasing your company's visibility through the exhibition of your products and solutions. With a strong presence in the Latin American coatings sector, the event offers direct contact with professionals in the paint industry, brand exposure and strengthening, and opportunities to find new customers in an exclusive environment. It's also a platform to strengthen existing relationships and build new partnerships.

 

Pesticide products include Acrylate, Glycol And Alcohol, Acetate, Amine and Amide, Alcohol Ester, Anhydride, Glycol Ether and Acetate, Glycol and Alcohol, Ketone, Epoxy Resin, Coating and painting Additives, Defoamer, Leveling Agent, Wetting Agent, Dispersant Agent, Photoinitiator & UV Monomers and Other.

 

About GOLIATH


GOLIATH, a professional trading company specializing in industrial chemicals, active pharmaceutical ingredients, food and feed additives, personal care, and agricultural chemicals, will showcase its wide range of products and services at these events. With over 1,000 products, GOLIATH is well-equipped to provide domestic and foreign customers with one-stop services, including professional procurement, sales and marketing, warehousing and logistics, quality assurance, and registration.

 

Take advantage of the opportunity to witness the latest innovations in agriculture and chemicals at these upcoming events. GOLIATH looks forward to meeting you at their booths and sharing their expertise and solutions. Stay tuned for more updates and highlights from these exhibitions in November.
