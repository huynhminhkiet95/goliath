Tianjin Chengyi Shines at Two Major Trade Shows in September 2024

Sep. 30, 2024

Share:

In September 2024, GOLIATH Chemical Trading Company showcased its commitment to innovation and excellence by participating in two prominent international trade shows: the Concrete Show South East Asia and In-Cosmetics Latin America.


Concrete Show South East Asia

Date: September 11-14, 2024

Location: Jakarta International Expo, Indonesia


Recognized as the premier event for the concrete and construction sector in the region, the Concrete Show South East Asia attracted thousands of industry professionals, manufacturers, and suppliers from around the world. This year, over 200 exhibitors showcased cutting-edge technology and sustainable solutions. GOLIATH leveraged this platform to demonstrate its dedication to quality and innovation.


At the exhibition, GOLIATH proudly presented its key products, including:


  • Accelerating Agents: Triisopropanolamine, Calcium Formate

  • Anti-freezing Agents: Sodium Formate, Sodium Acetate

  • Retarders: Hydroxyethylmethylcellulose, Hydroxypropyl Methyl Cellulose


GOLIATH offers a comprehensive range of construction additives, enhancing the performance and durability of building materials, including:


  • Accelerating Agent: Speeds up concrete curing for faster project completion.

  • Adhesive: Provides strong bonding for various applications.

  • Air Entraining Agent: Improves workability and freeze-thaw resistance.

  • Antifoaming Agent: Reduces foam in concrete mixes.

  • Hydrophobic Agent: Increases water resistance.

  • Superplasticizer: Enhances flowability while reducing water content.



The event also featured insightful seminars and workshops led by industry experts, addressing key trends and challenges, further engaging GOLIATH with potential clients and partners.




In-Cosmetics Latin America

Date: September 25-26, 2024

Location: São Paulo, Brazil


In-Cosmetics Latin America is the leading event for personal care ingredients in the region, focusing on innovation, sustainability, and product development. This year's exhibition attracted over 300 exhibitors, providing a platform for exploring the latest trends and technologies.


GOLIATH showcased its extensive range of high-quality personal care chemicals, including:


  • Emollients: Cyclopentasiloxane, Dimethicone

  • Oleochemicals: Stearyl Alcohol

  • Sunscreen Agents: Ethylhexyl Triazone, Octyl Salicylate, Octyl Methoxycinnamate


Additional products included:


  • Cationic Conditioner: Enhances hair manageability.

  • Thickener: Increases viscosity for improved texture.

  • Emulsifier: Facilitates mixing of oil and water phases.

  • Hair Transformation and Depilatory: Formulations that facilitate hair removal and transformation.

  • Preservative and Disinfectant: Ensures product safety and longevity by preventing microbial growth.

  • Rheology Modifier: Adjusts the flow properties of formulations for optimal performance.

  • Surfactants (Ampholytic): Versatile surfactants that can function in both anionic and cationic environments.

  • Sensory Agent: Enhances the sensory experience of personal care products.

  • Silicone: Provides smoothness and shine, commonly used in hair and skin care products.

  • Surfactants (Anionic): Effective for cleansing and foaming properties.

  • Surfactants (Nonionic): Mild surfactants suitable for sensitive skin formulations.

  • Vitamin: Nutrients added for skin health and wellness.

  • Whitening Agent: Enhances skin tone and brightness in cosmetic products.


The event offered numerous networking opportunities, allowing GOLIATH to build strong connections and gather insights into market trends and consumer preferences.





Future Outlook


Through participation in these two trade shows, GOLIATH reinforced its extensive presence in the chemical sector and strengthened relationships within the industry. Positive feedback from customers and partners has solidified its competitive position in chemical sales.


With over 1,000 products across five major sectors, GOLIATH Group is dedicated to professional procurement, sales and marketing, warehousing logistics, quality assurance, and regulatory registration services for clients worldwide. Looking ahead, GOLIATH remains committed to technological innovation and market expansion, driving the development of new solutions to meet evolving customer needs.


GOLIATH's successful participation in these events marks a significant step in solidifying its reputation as a trusted supplier in the global chemical market.
