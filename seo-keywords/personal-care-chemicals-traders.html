Personal Care Chemical Distributor​

About TJCY


Welcome to TJCY, we are a personal care chemicals distributor, committed to being your trusted partner in the personal care chemicals trading field.


At TJCY, we understand the importance of sourcing high-quality ingredients for personal care formulations. That's why we offer a comprehensive range of chemicals carefully selected to meet the diverse needs of our clients. From surfactants and emulsifiers to preservatives and specialty additives, we have everything you need to create innovative and effective personal care products.


What sets TJCY apart is our unwavering dedication to customer satisfaction. Our team of experts works tirelessly to ensure that every product we offer meets the highest standards of quality and safety. Whether you're a small-scale artisanal producer or a large-scale manufacturer, you can trust TJCY to provide you with the personalized service and support you need to succeed.


But our commitment doesn't stop there. At TJCY, we're also passionate about staying ahead of the curve when it comes to industry trends and innovations. We continuously monitor the market to identify emerging technologies and ingredients, allowing us to offer our clients the latest advancements in personal care chemistry.


When you choose TJCY as your personal care chemicals distributor, you're not just getting a supplier – you're gaining a strategic partner dedicated to helping you achieve your business goals. Experience the TJCY difference today and discover why we're the preferred choice for personal care professionals worldwide.


Personal Care Business Unit


Our personal care department collaborate with some factories through OEM/ODM, we strictly control the production process and product quality, and produce products with our own brand.We implement lSO 90011,lSO 14001 and OHSAS 18001 management and are devoted to providing our own high-quality personal care ingredients to customers. In the meantime, we build strategic partnership with some manufacturers in China, Malaysia and India. We also export some personal care ingredients of these partners to the worldwide markets.



Our Service


  • Technical support: 

Our own laboratory and skilled staffs will provide professional technical and formulation support to customers. 

  • Expertise Sourcing and marketing: 

Our commercial staffs have extensive experience and knowledge of personal care market. We are able to provide up to date supply trends and cost information on products to our clients, so that they may make informed and timely buying decisions & purchase. 

  • Delivery on time: 

One own warehouses in Xingang port and three contracted warehouses in Shanghai Qingdao and Guangzhou port will support us to provide our customers with combination of multiple products per FCL and on time delivery. 

  • QA and QC Support: 

Our QA department conducts strict audits ofour approved suppliers annually in accordance withlSO,GMP, EHS,FSSC requirements. Ourregular products are sampled and tested by our QC department each batch.
