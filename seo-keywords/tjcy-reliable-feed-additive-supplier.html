GOLIATH – Reliable Feed Additive Supplier

GOLIATH is a leading feed additive supplier dedicated to enhancing animal health, nutrition, and performance through science-based feed solutions. With decades of experience in the chemical and life science industry, we supply a comprehensive range of high-quality feed additives to global distributors, feed mills, and livestock producers.


As a global feed additive supplier, we serve over 80 countries with consistent quality, responsive service, and a commitment to regulatory compliance. Our products are widely used in poultry, swine, cattle, aquaculture, and pet food applications.


Your Trusted Feed Additive Supplier – One Source, Multiple Solutions

GOLIATH delivers a diverse portfolio of feed additives that help improve feed efficiency, promote growth, prevent disease, and support gut health. As your full-service feed additive supplier, we offer

Amino Acids

We supply essential amino acids such as L-Lysine, DL-Methionine, and L-Threonine, which are vital for protein synthesis and optimal animal growth.


Vitamins

Our vitamin range includes fat- and water-soluble vitamins like Vitamin A, D3, E, K3, B-complex, and C. These nutrients are critical for metabolism, immunity, and reproduction.


Minerals

As a reliable feed additive supplier, we provide both organic and inorganic mineral sources, including zinc sulfate, copper sulfate, ferrous sulfate, and trace mineral blends to meet species-specific requirements.


Enzymes

We offer feed-grade enzymes such as phytase, xylanase, and protease, designed to enhance nutrient digestibility and reduce anti-nutritional factors in animal diets.


Probiotics and Yeast Products

Our probiotics and yeast-based additives, including Saccharomyces cerevisiae and Bacillus subtilis, support gut microbiota balance and improve feed intake and conversion rates.


Preservatives and Antioxidants

GOLIATH offers feed preservatives like propionic acid and BHT to prevent mold, rancidity, and spoilage, ensuring the long-term stability of feed formulations.


Acidifiers and Gut Health Enhancers

Organic acid blends and butyrates are used to improve gut pH, enhance microbial balance, and reduce pathogen load, supporting healthier animals without antibiotics.


Other Functional Additives

From flavoring agents and binders to toxin binders and emulsifiers, we supply specialty additives to improve palatability, feed texture, and safety.
