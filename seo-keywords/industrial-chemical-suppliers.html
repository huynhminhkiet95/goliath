Industrial Chemical Supplier | GOLIATH


GOLIATH has more than 24 years of experience as a industrial chemical supplier and distributor, focusing on providing comprehensive solutions to the growing raw material needs of various industries. Our vision is to provide a full range of services to our customers and hence we offer a wide range of products to meet the demand for chemical commodities across the globe. As a trusted chemical distributor, we are committed to providing our customers with a seamless supply chain experience, ensuring they have easy access to the chemicals they need. Our experienced team leaders, supported by global teams, work around the clock to deliver optimized supply chain services. We help our clients' businesses achieve competitive advantage through a deep understanding of market solutions and industry trends. With branches and overseas offices in Brazil, Canada, Sweden, the United States and the Netherlands, we can safely and competitively provide our customers with the products they need.


As one of the world's leading industrial chemical supplier and distributor, organizations from around the world rely on GOLIATH to deliver the highest quality chemicals available today.
