Trusted Chemical Wholesalers for One-Stop Sourcing | GOLIATH

Looking for a reliable partner in the chemical industry? GOLIATH is your trusted chemical wholesaler, offering a one-stop sourcing solution for over 1,000 types of raw materials across multiple industries. With global warehouses, certified products, and a professional procurement team, we help businesses around the world secure consistent, compliant, and competitively priced chemical supplies.

 

Why Choose GOLIATH as Your Chemical Wholesaler?

 

GOLIATH has been a leading chemical wholesaler and distributor for years, trusted by thousands of manufacturers, processors, and formulation companies worldwide.

 

Diverse Portfolio: Over 1,000 products across industrial chemicals, food and feed ingredients, personal care raw materials, active pharmaceutical ingredients (APIs), and agrochemicals.

 

Global Reach: Multiple overseas warehouses ensure faster delivery and reduced supply chain risks.

 

Professional Procurement Team: We work closely with thousands of vetted manufacturers to offer a tailored sourcing experience.

 

Certified Quality: All products meet GMP, ISO, FSSC, and other international standards, passing strict audits before shipment.

 

Whether you are a manufacturer, distributor, or private label brand, GOLIATH can streamline your procurement and reduce your costs.

 

Our Product Categories

 

We specialize in a broad range of chemical raw materials tailored to diverse industries. Discover our five major product lines:

 

1. Industrial Chemicals

We offer a wide selection of bulk industrial chemicals used in Paints and coatings, water treatment, industrial cleaning, building additives, etc. Reliable and competitively priced, our solutions support large-scale manufacturing needs.

 

2. Food/ Feed Ingredients

From sweeteners to acidulants and preservatives, we supply food-grade and feed-grade ingredients that comply with global safety and quality standards. HACCP and FSSC documentation available upon request.

 

3. Personal Care Raw Materials

We deliver functional ingredients such as surfactants, emollients, and emulsifiers for personal care, cosmetics, and household cleaning products—backed by safety data and formulation support.

 

4. Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs)

Our API portfolio covers anti-infection, cardiovascular, analgesic/antipyretic, and other therapeutic classes. All APIs meet GMP compliance, with documentation such as DMF, CEP available for certain markets.

 

5. Agrochemicals

We provide raw materials and intermediates for fertilizers, pesticides, herbicides, and plant growth regulators. Our agrochemical supply is tailored for both local distributors and international formulators.

 

Global Warehousing & Efficient Delivery

 

GOLIATH maintains strategic warehouse locations overseas, helping you save on lead time, freight costs, and customs clearance. By keeping stock close to your market, we ensure you never run out of essential materials.

 

Our logistics team works with leading freight partners to offer door-to-door solutions, with full documentation and shipment tracking for peace of mind.

 

Certified Quality Assurance

 

At GOLIATH, quality is non-negotiable. Every product we offer goes through strict quality control processes, including third-party testing, COAs, and compliance checks.

 

We only work with suppliers that meet our stringent vendor audit program, ensuring:

 

GMP (Good Manufacturing Practice) compliance

ISO-certified production

FAMI-QS adherence for food ingredients

Regulatory documentation for APIs and other sensitive categories

 

This gives our customers the confidence and traceability they need, no matter the industry.

 

Customized Sourcing & OEM Services

 

Need more than just off-the-shelf products? Our team supports custom sourcing, blending, and OEM/private label services tailored to your specifications.

 

We can help you:

 

1. Customized formulation

2. Customized packaging and assembly methods

3. Customized product combination solutions

4. From laboratory to production scale

 

With flexible MOQs and responsive support, GOLIATH is your supply chain partner from start to finish.

FAQs – About Our Chemical Wholesale Services

 

Q: What certifications do your products have?

A:We strictly comply with all regulatory requirements of international and local markets. Our suppliers are audited by our company's Quality Inspection and Regulatory Department according to ISO, GMP, FAMI-QS, EUGMP, FDA, CEP and other quality standards.

 

Q: Do you support sample trials?

A: Yes. We provide you with free samples and professional technical support to ensure that the formula meets your needs.

 

Q: Do you ship globally?

A: Absolutely. We serve clients in over 70 countries with reliable export documentation and customs support.

 

Q: How fast can you deliver?

A: Delivery times depend on product type and destination. Our company's delivery chain consists of the Operation Department, Logistics Department and Document Department, which synchronizes the order status with you in real time and ensures arrival at the agreed time.
