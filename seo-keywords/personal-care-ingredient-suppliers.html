Personal Care Ingredient Suppliers | GOLIATH

GOLIATH focuses on becoming a professional supplier in the field of personal care chemicals and has established close cooperative relationships with more than 1,000 chemical factories over the past many years. 


We provide hot-selling whitening agents, emollients, sunscreens and other products, and are committed to providing customers with green, sustainable, safe, efficient and competitive chemicals. 

Our services are global and our local presence is strengthened. Branch offices are located in more than 70 countries and regions around the world, including branches in the United States, the Netherlands, Brazil, Russia and other places, and representative offices in Canada and Sweden. At the same time, we have warehouses in Tianjin, Qingdao and Shanghai Port, and have signed contracts with more than 50 freight forwarders to provide reliable and rapid distribution services to global customers. 

We also provide warehousing and logistics services in Brazil, Russia, the Netherlands, the United States and other places.
