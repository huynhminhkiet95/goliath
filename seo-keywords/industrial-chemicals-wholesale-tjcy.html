Industrial Chemicals wholesale|GOLIATH

GOLIATH Group is an international trading company focusing on wholesale of industrial chemicals. With more than 25 years of industry experience, its products cover multiple fields such as Construction, paints and coatings, water treatment, industrial cleaning chemicals, etc., and it is committed to providing high-quality chemical solutions to global customers.

 

Industrial Chemicals Product Series


GOLIATH has a wide range of industrial chemicals to meet the diverse needs of different industries. The main products include:


Basic chemicals

Basic chemicals are fundamental building blocks used across industries.These chemicals are produced in large volumes and form the foundation of the global chemical industry. such as formic acid, phosphoric acid, oxalic acid, etc., are widely used in various industrial processes.


Ammonium Chloride
Sodium Fluosilicate
Calcium Hypochlorite
Benzyl Alcohol
Vinyl toluene
Methyl Ethyl Ketone

Construction chemical additives

Construction chemical additives enhance the performance of building materials. These additives improve concrete strength, workability, durability, and resistance, ensuring better construction quality and longer service life of structures. such as sodium tripolyphosphate (STPP), sodium hexametaphosphate (SHMP), sodium tripolyphosphate (TSP), etc.


Calcium Formate
Sodium Formate
Sodium tripolyphosphate
Sodium hexametaphosphate
Sodium tripolyphosphate
Hydroxyethylmethylcellulose

Water treatment chemicals

Water treatment chemicals purify and condition water for industrial, municipal, and residential use. These chemicals remove impurities, kill pathogens, and adjust water properties to meet safety and environmental standards. such as Tetrakis(hydroxymethyl)phosphonium sulfate (THPS)、Glutaraldehyde、Sodium Chlorite, etc.

Poly Aluminum Chloride
Ion exchange Resin
Amino Trimethylene
Phosphonic Acid
Sodium Chlorite
Glutaraldehyde
Monosodium phosphate

Paint and coating raw chemicals

Paint and coating raw are essential for creating protective and decorative finishes on surfaces. These chemicals provide color, durability, corrosion resistance, and adhesion properties in coatings used across automotive, industrial, and architectural sectors. such as polyvinyl alcohol (PVA), butyl acrylate, etc.


2-Hydroxypropyl Methacrylate
Methyl Methacrylate
Sebacic acid
Texanol
Trimethylolpropane
Methyl Ethyl Ketoxime

Detergents and surfactants

Detergents and surfactants are key in cleaning products. Surfactants reduce surface tension, allowing detergents to lift dirt and oils. Used in household, industrial, and personal care products, they provide foaming, emulsifying, and dispersing functions, enhancing cleaning effectiveness and formulation performance.such as sodium dodecylbenzene sulfonate (SDBS), alkyl polyglycoside (APG),Sodium Lauryl Ether Sulfate(SLES) , etc.


Ethylenediaminetetraacetic Acid Tetrasodium Salt
Ethylenediaminetetraacetic Acid Disodium Salt
Sodium Metasilicate
Dipropylene Glycol
Sodium Lauryl Ether Sulfate
alkyl polyglycoside

About GOLIATH Group

 

GOLIATH Group was founded in 2000 and is headquartered in Taiyuan, China, with branches in the United States, Canada, Brazil, the Netherlands and other places. The company's main product lines are industrial chemicals, pharmaceutical raw materials and intermediates, food and feed additives, personal care raw materials and agricultural chemicals, with a total of more than 1,000 products.

 

We are committed to providing one-stop services to global customers, including professional procurement, sales and marketing support, warehousing and logistics, quality assurance (QA/QC) and regulatory registration.

Product series: 5 categories, more than 1,000 products

Number of customers: 1,000+

Service countries: 81

Cooperating factories: Established business cooperation with 1,666 well-known and reliable factories

 

GOLIATH has obtained ISO 9001 quality management system certification, ISO 14001 environmental management system certification, ISO 45001 occupational health and safety management system certification and FAMI-QS certification to ensure high standards of product quality and service.

 

One-stop service advantages


Choosing GOLIATH, you will enjoy full-range service support:

 

Professional procurement team: Goliath has a professional procurement team and maintains long-term cooperative relations with thousands of manufacturers at home and abroad. Choose suitable suppliers with rich experience and provide solutions to customers with valuable guidance, knowledge and service awareness.

 

Sales and market support: We are deeply involved in the chemical industry, conduct in-depth research on the chemical market, select superior products, and provide professional product solutions based on professional knowledge.

 

Warehousing and logistics: GOLIATH Logistics Department integrates sea, air and rail transportation. Each business starts from placing an order, including inquiry, booking, packing, customs declaration, port collection, loading, unloading at the destination port, and notifying customers to pick up goods. We track the whole process and update the dynamics at any time until the goods arrive in the hands of customers.

 

Quality assurance (QA/QC): We are equipped with advanced testing equipment and professional and experienced testing personnel. Each batch of key products must be sampled and tested in accordance with the sampling SOP, and can only be shipped after the test is qualified. All test samples are retained for one year after the expiration of the validity period for subsequent follow-up investigations.

 

Regulatory Registration Support: The Regulatory Department is always committed to providing customers with professional registration documents. We comply with all regulatory requirements of your local market and can help you register with USFDA, ANVISA and MAPA.

 

Our customers are located in many countries and regions around the world, covering a variety of industries such as Industry, food, cosmetics, pharmaceuticals. They choose GOLIATH not only because of our product quality, but also because of our professional, efficient, and reliable one-stop service.
