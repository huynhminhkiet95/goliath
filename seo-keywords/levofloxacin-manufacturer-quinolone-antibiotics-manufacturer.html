Levofloxacin Manufacturer | Quinolone Antibiotics Manufacturer

In the treatment of respiratory tract, urinary tract, intestinal and skin infections, quinolone antibiotics have become the mainstream anti-infection treatment method in the world due to their broad spectrum, high efficiency and strong tissue penetration. In particular, the new generation of fluoroquinolones represented by levofloxacin are widely used in hospitals and primary medical institutions. As a professional supplier of quinolone raw materials, TJCY can provide multiple gradient product options, strict quality control and registration support suitable for the global market.

 

Our Key Advantages

 

Mature Production Process with Strict Impurity Control:

TJCY provides Levofloxacin in multiple grades that meet USP, EP, and CP standards. Our products are designed for both sterile and non-sterile applications, ensuring quality for pharmaceutical manufacturers.

 

Comprehensive Regulatory Support:

For select products, we offer DMF, CEP, and other necessary regulatory documentation, assisting our clients in efficiently navigating global market registrations for raw materials.

 

Established Global Customer Base:

As a trusted raw material supplier, TJCY has been supplying quinolone antibiotics to the global market and has established long-term partnerships with pharmaceutical companies.

 

Reliable and Consistent Supply:

Leveraging stable access to upstream intermediates and a multi-site production network, TJCY ensures continuous and on-time bulk supply of Levofloxacin raw materials to meet customer production demands.

First-Generation Quinolones

These drugs primarily target Gram-negative bacteria with a narrow spectrum of activity, used mostly for urinary tract infections (UTIs). They are less commonly used in modern clinical practice.

Primary Applications: Urinary tract infections

Nalidixic Acid
Cinoxacin

Second-Generation Fluoroquinolones

This generation exhibits enhanced activity against Gram-negative bacteria, making them effective for treating urinary tract and gastrointestinal infections.

Primary Applications: Urinary tract infections, Gastrointestinal infections

Norfloxacin
Ciprofloxacin
Cinoxacin

Third-Generation Fluoroquinolones

These drugs have stronger activity against both Gram-positive and Gram-negative bacteria, making them widely used for respiratory infections, urinary tract infections, and skin infections.

Primary Applications: Respiratory tract infections, Skin infections, Urinary tract infections

Enoxacin
Ofloxacin
Levofloxacin
Fleroxacin

Fourth-Generation Fluoroquinolones

The fourth generation of fluoroquinolones offers the broadest antimicrobial spectrum, effective against both aerobic and anaerobic bacteria. They are used for treating complex infections, including resistant strains.

Primary Applications: Respiratory tract infections, Complex infections, Resistant bacterial infections

Moxifloxacin
Gemifloxacin
Gatifloxacin
Delafloxacin

New-Generation Quinolones

These new-generation quinolones provide ultra-broad-spectrum activity, targeting multi-drug resistant bacteria. They are mainly used for treating infections caused by resistant strains.

Primary Applications: Resistant bacterial infections

Sitafloxacin
Nemonoxacin

Overview of hot-selling products

 

1. Levofloxacin

Non-sterile grade, sterile grade (injection grade), L-isomer control ≤0.5%.

Supply can be provided according to EP / USP / JP / CP requirements.

Applied to tablets, eye drops, injections and other preparations.

 

2. Ciprofloxacin

Strong activity, suitable for diarrhea, urinary tract infection and other indications.

TJCY provides pharmaceutical grade powder, customizable particle size, supporting solubility and residual solvent reports.

 

3. Moxifloxacin

More suitable for multi-drug resistant bacterial infections, commonly used in community-acquired pneumonia.

Provide high-purity API and support customization of sterile raw materials.

 

4. Delafloxacin / Sitafloxacin / Nemonoxacin

Belong to the new quinolone class, suitable for Gram-positive bacteria resistant to traditional fluoroquinolones.

TJCY can provide small batch samples according to the needs of customers in the R&D stage, with supporting impurity spectrum.
