Agrochemicals Products: Advanced Solutions for Agriculture

As a global leader in the chemical industry, TJCY offers a wide range of high-quality agrochemical products designed to enhance agricultural productivity and sustainability. Our portfolio includes Lambda-cyhalothrin TC, Bifenthrin TC, Diuron TC, Imidacloprid TC, and Chlorobenzamide TC. Each product is carefully sourced to ensure effectiveness, safety, and reliability.


Lambda-cyhalothrin TC

Lambda-cyhalothrin TC is a broad-spectrum insecticide used to control a variety of pests in crops such as cotton, cereals, vegetables, and fruits. It works by disrupting the nervous system of insects, leading to their rapid elimination. TJCY’s Lambda-cyhalothrin TC is sourced from trusted manufacturers, ensuring high efficacy and safety for agricultural use.


Bifenthrin TC

Bifenthrin TC is another powerful insecticide that targets a wide range of insects in various crops, including ornamental plants and turf. It is known for its long-lasting residual activity and effectiveness in pest control. Our Bifenthrin TC is produced under strict quality controls, ensuring it meets the highest standards for agricultural applications.


Diuron TC

Diuron TC is a versatile herbicide used to control a broad spectrum of weeds in crops like sugarcane, cotton, and fruit orchards. It works by inhibiting photosynthesis in target plants, leading to their gradual elimination. TJCY’s Diuron TC is sourced from reputable manufacturers, ensuring its reliability and effectiveness in weed management.


Imidacloprid TC

Imidacloprid TC is a widely used insecticide known for its systemic action and effectiveness against a range of sucking and chewing pests. It is particularly effective in protecting crops such as rice, maize, and vegetables. TJCY ensures that our Imidacloprid TC meets the stringent quality requirements necessary for safe and effective pest control.


Chlorobenzamide TC

Chlorobenzamide TC is a fungicide used to protect crops from various fungal diseases, ensuring healthy plant growth and higher yields. It is effective in controlling diseases in crops such as fruits, vegetables, and cereals. Our Chlorobenzamide TC is sourced from reliable manufacturers, ensuring consistent quality and performance.
