Nutritional Supplement Supplier – Bioactive Ingredients for Health

In a world increasingly focused on preventive healthcare, physical performance, and active aging, nutritional supplements have become more than just a trend—they’re a cornerstone of modern health strategies. Consumers today demand clean-label, science-backed ingredients that support immunity, brain health, cardiovascular function, and energy metabolism. This shift is driving global brands to seek reliable nutritional supplement suppliers who can deliver both efficacy and compliance.

 

What Are Nutritional Supplements?

 

Nutritional supplements, also referred to as dietary supplements, are products formulated to provide essential nutrients that may be missing or insufficient in a person’s regular diet. These include vitamins, minerals, amino acids, antioxidants, and other bioactive compounds that support various aspects of health—from boosting immunity and brain function to improving metabolism and maintaining cardiovascular well-being.

 

From sports nutrition and dietary supplements to functional foods and beverages, the demand for bioavailable and multifunctional ingredients continues to grow. As a global nutritional supplement supplier, TJCY offers a wide range of bioactive compounds designed to meet the evolving needs of wellness-driven product development.

 

Our Featured Nutritional Ingredients

 

We offer a broad range of nutritional supplements designed to support health, growth, and performance across food, feed, and pharmaceutical industries. Our key product categories include amino acids, vitamins, minerals, and other bioactive compounds.

Amino Acids

Amino acids are the building blocks of protein and play a crucial role in muscle development, immune function, metabolism, and tissue repair. They are widely used in sports nutrition, clinical formulations, dietary supplements, and animal feed to support recovery and performance.

 

Typical applications: protein powders, energy drinks, parenteral nutrition, amino acid blends for livestock.

Vitamins

Vitamins are essential micronutrients that support a variety of physiological functions including immunity, vision, bone health, and antioxidant defense. Both fat-soluble and water-soluble vitamins are used in food fortification, health supplements, and pharmaceutical products.

 

Typical applications: multivitamin supplements, fortified beverages, infant nutrition, immune support capsules.

Minerals

Minerals are critical for maintaining electrolyte balance, bone development, nerve transmission, and enzymatic activities. Our mineral ingredients are characterized by high purity, stability, and bioavailability, making them suitable for human nutrition, veterinary use, and feed additives.

 

Typical applications: mineral tablets, electrolyte drinks, animal nutrition premixes, therapeutic food formulations.

Bioactive Compounds

Beyond basic nutrition, certain bioactive ingredients offer functional benefits such as enhanced metabolism, improved liver health, and athletic performance support. These compounds are increasingly used in high-performance nutrition and clinical dietary solutions.

 

Typical applications: sports supplements, liver support blends, clinical nutrition formulas.
