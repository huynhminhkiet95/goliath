Sweetener Supplier – Comprehensive Solutions for Functional Taste Design

In modern product development, sweetness isn't just about taste—it's about function, perception, and health. Whether it's a zero-calorie soft drink, a diabetic-friendly snack, or a pharmaceutical syrup, the choice of sweetener can directly impact consumer experience, regulatory approval, and product success.

 

Sweeteners today are expected to do more than replace sugar. They must deliver clean flavor profiles, withstand thermal processing, improve mouthfeel, and support claims like "sugar-free," "low glycemic," or "keto-friendly." As dietary preferences and health concerns evolve, manufacturers are increasingly turning to specialized sweetener suppliers to meet their formulation goals without compromising on taste or compliance.

 

At GOLIATH, we provide a complete portfolio of high-performance sweeteners—ranging from high-intensity synthetics to natural polyols—to help formulators create balanced, stable, and appealing products across a wide spectrum of industries.

Our sweeteners are widely used in:

 

1. Sugar-Free Beverages

Sucralose, Ace-K, and Aspartame are frequently blended in zero-calorie and sugar-free beverages to mimic the sweet profile of sucrose while improving taste balance. These high-intensity sweeteners are highly stable and perform well in carbonated drinks, flavored waters, functional beverages, and energy drinks.

 

2. Baked Goods and Functional Snacks

Erythritol and Ace-K are ideal for low-sugar baking applications, including cookies, muffins, and nutrition bars. Erythritol contributes bulk and texture similar to sugar, while maintaining a clean sweetness, making it suitable for keto-friendly snacks and functional foods.

 

3. Nutritional Supplements and Health Foods

Aspartame and Sorbitol (70% solution) are commonly used in vitamin syrups, protein shakes, and dietary gummies, offering moderate sweetness with excellent solubility. These sweeteners are compatible with active ingredients and meet the needs of diabetic and health-conscious consumers.

 

4. Oral and Personal Care Products

Saccharin Sodium and Sorbitol are widely used in oral care formulations such as toothpaste, mouthwash, and chewing gum. Their non-cariogenic properties, long-lasting sweetness, and flavor-masking ability make them suitable for daily-use personal care products.

 

5. Pharmaceutical Formulations

D-Glucose Monohydrate and Sorbitol are preferred in oral drug formulations, including syrups, chewable tablets, and effervescent powders. Sucralose and Aspartame are also suitable for pediatric pharmaceuticals, providing pleasant sweetness without the use of sugar.

Sodium Cyclamate: High-Intensity, Stable, and Cost-Effective

 

Sodium Cyclamate is 30–50 times sweeter than sucrose and remains highly stable under heat and acidic conditions, making it ideal for large-scale beverage and canned food production. It's commonly used in:

Diet soft drinks

Baked goods

Fermented or pickled foods

Tabletop sweeteners

 

As a sweetener supplier, we provide high-purity, food-grade sodium cyclamate with reliable solubility and sensory consistency, suited for hot and cold applications alike.

 

Sorbitol 70% Solution: Sweetener + Humectant for Moisture Control

 

Sorbitol is a sugar alcohol that functions as both a mild sweetener and a moisture-retention agent. It is widely used in:

Sugar-free chewing gum

Diabetic-friendly confectionery

Toothpaste and oral hygiene products

Lotions and cosmetics

 

GOLIATH's sorbitol solution ensures stable viscosity, high microbial purity, and reliable performance in formulations requiring long shelf life and texture stability.

 

Sucralose: Zero-Calorie Sweetener with Sugar-Like Taste

 

Sucralose offers 600 times the sweetness of sugar, with no aftertaste and excellent heat resistance—ideal for baked goods and processed foods. Key applications include:

Flavored dairy beverages

Baked snacks and protein bars

Fortified nutritional supplements

Instant beverage mixes

 

Our sucralose meets stringent USP, FCC, and EP standards, and is available in multiple mesh sizes for flexible blending.

 

Dextrose Monohydrate: Naturally Occurring Glucose for Energy and Mild Sweetness

 

Dextrose provides immediate energy and is commonly used where rapid carbohydrate absorption is desired. It is found in:

Sports and rehydration drinks

Bakery and confectionery products

Nutritional supplements and medical formulations

Chewable tablets and lozenges

 

As a reliable sweetener supplier, we ensure low-moisture, high-purity dextrose that performs consistently across applications.

 

Erythritol: Natural, Non-Glycemic, and Clean-Label

 

Erythritol is a non-caloric sugar alcohol with about 70% of the sweetness of sugar and a negligible effect on blood glucose. Its cooling effect and clean label appeal make it ideal for:

Keto-friendly and diabetic-safe products

Low-sugar chocolate and baked items

Functional beverages and powdered blends

Oral care and sugar-free candy

 

We offer non-GMO, crystalline erythritol with fine particle control and excellent solubility.

 

Acesulfame-K (Ace-K): High-Intensity and Heat-Stable Synergist

 

Ace-K is approximately 200 times sweeter than sugar and is often combined with other sweeteners to enhance overall taste perception. It is used in:

Zero-calorie soft drinks

Dairy desserts

Sugar-free lozenges

Functional beverages
