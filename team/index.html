BUSINESS CENTER

BUSINESS CENTER 1

Business Center 1 focuses on the field of industrial chemicals. We are committed to providing high-performance, customized chemical solutions to global industrial customers. We focus on niche markets and continue to create higher value for customers with our professional product knowledge and industry experience.

The core product series covers the following four areas: Construction Additives, Painting & Coating, Water Treatment, Industry Cleaning.

BUSINESS CENTER 2

Business Center 2 focuses on three business sectors: Food & Feed Additives, Personal Care & Home Care and Pharmaceutical. We are committed to providing high-standard raw material support and solutions for life health and quality life.

Business Center 2 strictly follows the industry regulations and safety standards of various countries in the process of product development, quality control and market expansion, and has passed a number of international qualification certifications.

HUMAN RESOURCES DEPARTMENT
