Corporate Social Responsibility and Sustainability Commitment


Tianjin Chengyi International Trading Co., Ltd. firmly believes that our long-term success is intrinsically linked to the well-being of our employees, the communities we operate in, and the health of our planet. To fulfill our role as a responsible corporate citizen and in active response to the global call for sustainable development, we hereby publicly commit to consistently adhering to and promoting the following principles in our daily operations and business decisions:


1. Respect for Human Rights & Labor Standards

We are committed to upholding the human rights and dignity of all our employees by providing a fair, safe, and healthy working environment.

We prohibit the use of child labor and forced labor, and eliminate any form of discrimination and harassment.

We respect our employees legal rights to freedom of association and collective bargaining, and ensure employee care through open communication channels.


2. Environmental Protection & Green Operations

We commit to complying with all environmental regulations, striving to prevent pollution, conserve resources, and continuously improve resource efficiency.

We actively promote waste reduction, reuse, and recycling, and prioritize environmentally preferable products and services.


3. Ethical Business & Anti-Corruption

We uphold the highest standards of business ethics and maintain a zero-tolerance policy against all forms of corruption, extortion, and bribery.

We are committed to transparency in our business dealings and have established effective mechanisms for employees and business partners to report any unethical practices in confidence and without fear of retaliation.


4. Building a Sustainable Supply Chain

We expect and encourage our business partners and suppliers to adopt social and environmental responsibility standards consistent with the spirit of this commitment.

 

This commitment is endorsed by our top management and is publicly disclosed to all employees and the public. We will ensure the implementation of these principles through regular audits, and training, striving for continuous improvement.
