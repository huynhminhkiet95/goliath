GOLIATH Begins a New Journey in 2026

Mar. 12, 2026

Share:

As a new year begins, GOLIATH enters 2026 with renewed energy, greater confidence, and a clear commitment to the future. This special season, filled with momentum and hope, marked an important moment for the company to reflect on past achievements, celebrate its people, and look ahead to a new stage of development.



Looking back, GOLIATH has always been guided by professionalism and integrity. Through steady progress in the chemical industry, the company has built a solid foundation, accumulated valuable experience, and strengthened its ability to serve customers with reliability and expertise. These achievements have not only supported the company’s growth over the years, but have also prepared GOLIATH for the opportunities and challenges of the future.

 

In 2026, GOLIATH will continue to move forward with a strategic focus on deeper specialization and broader market expansion. At the same time, the company has embraced a renewed set of core values: Professionalism, Integrity, Collaboration, Growth, and Breakthrough. These values will guide GOLIATH in its operations, team development, and long-term vision, helping unite employees across departments and functions as the company pursues new goals together.

 

A key part of the event was the presentation of GOLIATH’s corporate culture. Corporate culture is more than a set of ideas—it is the foundation of a company’s identity and the source of its long-term vitality. It reflects the original aspirations of the founders, the determination shown throughout the company’s journey, and the shared beliefs that continue to inspire progress. For GOLIATH, culture remains a powerful force that brings people together, strengthens commitment, and supports sustainable development.

 

The event also included a special recognition session honoring outstanding employees for their dedication and achievements in 2025. The past year was one of both perseverance and breakthrough. GOLIATH continued to deepen its presence in specialized segments, pursue innovation with determination, optimize its organizational structure, and focus its business on seven key industry sectors. At the same time, the company further strengthened its collaborative operating model across teams, creating stronger alignment and higher efficiency throughout the organization.

 

Throughout this journey, many employees stood out through exceptional performance, strong responsibility, and consistent professionalism. Their hard work and commitment embodied the spirit of GOLIATH and set an example for others across the company. By recognizing these team members, GOLIATH not only celebrated individual excellence, but also reinforced a culture that values dedication, accountability, and continuous improvement.

 

 

 

Another important highlight of the event was the strategic presentation delivered by Chairman An Xin. Through his interpretation of the company’s 2026 roadmap, all employees gained a clearer understanding of GOLIATH’s development priorities, key initiatives, and future path. The presentation helped build shared understanding across the organization and gave employees greater clarity about their own roles and responsibilities in the year ahead. More importantly, it strengthened confidence and inspired a stronger sense of mission as the company moves toward its next chapter.

 

 


The celebration concluded with a gala evening that brought everyone together in a joyful and uplifting atmosphere. As the company reflected on a year of hard work and shared achievement, the event became both a celebration of the past and a warm welcome to the future. The evening began with Chairman An Xin’s New Year address, setting the tone for a memorable program filled with energy, connection, and optimism.

 

Over the past year, GOLIATH has moved forward side by side, turning effort into achievement through teamwork and persistence. Looking ahead, the company is ready to continue writing a new chapter with confidence and determination. The strategic direction for 2026 is now clearly defined, and this annual gathering served not only as a celebration, but also as a powerful call to action—bringing people together, aligning goals, and igniting enthusiasm for the journey ahead.

 

With consensus strengthened and direction unified, GOLIATH is ready to embrace the new year with one heart and one purpose. In 2026, the company will continue to move forward together with its employees, partners, and customers to create an even brighter future.
