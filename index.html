01 Company Introduction

This is us, GOLIATH.

GOLIATH is a professional international trading company specializing in chemicals and pharmaceuticals, headquartered in Taiyuan China. We have branches and overseas offices in Brazil, Canada, Sweden, the United States and the Netherlands. With 24 years of continuous development, we have formed five business segments: industrial chemicals, pharmaceutical raw materials, food/feed additives, personal care ingredients and agrochemicals, total more than 1,000 products in our portfolio.

API
