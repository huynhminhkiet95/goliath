Pharmaceutical Raw Materials Supplier | GOLIATH

Feb. 27, 2025

Share:

Established in 1999, GOLIATH is a Hong Kong-registered international trading company headquartered in Taiyuan, Shanxi Province, China. As a leading Pharmaceutical Raw Materials Supplier, GOLIATH has grown steadily over the past two decades, establishing a dedicated team of professionals in six functional centers. One of the functional departments is the Pharmaceutical Business Unit, which specializes in raw material pharmaceutical related business. Our experienced team oversees not only product sales, research, and quality control but also handles product registration and regulatory compliance. 


To further enhance client support, GOLIATH has established a U.S.-based subsidiary, as well as representative offices in Brazil, the Netherlands, Canada, and two locations in Sweden.Today, we operate with a diverse portfolio of over 1,000 products, actively serving clients in more than 60 countries and regions.


1. One-Stop Service for International Clients


As a Pharmaceutical Raw Materials Supplier, GOLIATH provides a complete suite of services for clients worldwide, ensuring a seamless experience:


Sourcing: Our experienced procurement team selects suppliers with proven expertise, delivering valuable insights and customized solutions for clients.

Supplier Network: We maintain long-standing partnerships with thousands of domestic and international manufacturers, ensuring product quality and reliable sourcing.

Logistics: With warehouses in Xingang, Qingdao, Shanghai, São Paulo, Breda, and Toledo, we provide timely and efficient delivery with one-stop convenience.

Quality Assurance: All suppliers adhere to ISO, GMP, FSSC, and QA standards, ensuring consistent quality in our product offerings.

Quality Control: Our primary products are rigorously sampled and verified before distribution.

Regulatory Affairs (RA) Support: We provide comprehensive registration support, including ANVISA, EDQM, FDA, and MAPA registrations.


2. The uniqueness of GOLIATH as a supplier of Pharmaceutical Raw Materials


2.1 Experienced Pharmaceutical Business Unit


We started API business from 2003 originally in European market. Then expanded business to South America, mainly in Brazil. With 20 years experience, we build strategic partnership with many Chinese manufacturers and global pharmaceutical companies. Now we are enlarging our business footprint in more and more markets.

 

2.2 Professional RA Regulatory Registration Consulting


GOLIATH offers comprehensive RA regulatory registration consulting services to assist clients with drug registrations in the Brazilian, European, Russian, and Mexican markets. Our support spans the entire lifecycle of project registration, including tracking and maintenance.

 

Our experienced RA team specializes in local regulatory registration and consulting, covering agencies such as ANVISA, EDQM, COFEPRIS, MAPA, and more. We have successfully facilitated the registration of over 120 drugs in Brazil and Europe, as well as 19 MAPA registrations.

 

If you are interested in collaborating with us, we are fully prepared to provide tailored support to ensure the smooth progress of your business endeavors.
